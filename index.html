Why Thailand?

Thailand, the land of smiles

The legend has it that Thailand was named "Prathet Thai" by the locals, meaning "land of the free". Just like its name, Thailand is known for its warm hospitality and friendly people. However, it wasn't until the 20th century that Thailand became a popular destination for travelers seeking adventure and culture.

You can still see traces of ancient Thailand today. Beyond the bustling markets and vibrant street food scene, you can explore the stunning temples, lush jungles, and beautiful beaches that make Thailand a unique and diverse destination.
